How it works

From idea to
finished piece.

STEP 1

Share your idea

Tell Sakshi what you're picturing: the space it's for, rough size, colours, and how you'd like to use it.

STEP 2

A proposal comes back

A form, glaze direction and rough timeline, shaped around what coil building can actually do well.

STEP 3

Building & firing

Your piece is hand coiled, then fired and glazed. Most commissions take around three to four weeks.

STEP 4

Delivered or collected

Ready to pick up from the studio, or shipped carefully if you're not in Mumbai.

For designers & spaces

Interior collaborations.

Sakshi works with interior designers and studios to bring coil built forms into larger, immersive spaces: a set of vessels for a lobby, a run of custom tableware for a restaurant, or a sculptural piece anchoring a room. Happy to talk through scale, timelines and production capacity for larger orders.
