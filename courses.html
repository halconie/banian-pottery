How the long course works

Ten days, start to finish.

DAYS 1 TO 3

Foundations

Getting comfortable with clay, wedging, and the basic building or centring moves, depending on the track you pick.

DAYS 4 TO 7

Building your pieces

Working on two or three pieces of your own, with one-on-one guidance as forms take shape.

DAYS 8 TO 10

Finishing and glazing

Trimming, refining, and choosing glazes before everything goes in for firing.

AROUND DAY 20

Pieces ready

Your finished, fired and glazed work is ready to collect from the studio.
